My Must Try Restaurants in Saipan!

I love food. It can be difficult choosing a place where to eat your breakfast, lunch, and dinner. This website will be about my top favorite restaurants in Saipan.

Best Places to Dine:

  1. Ocean Star Restaurant
  2. MORAE Kitchen and Bar
  3. Tom Yum
  4. J's Restaurant
  5. KFC

Ocean Star Restaurant | Garapan

Ocean Star Restaurant is a Chinese and Filipino restaurant located in Garapan. It is one of my favorite restaurants becaus they have a wide variety of dishes to choose from. The food is always fresh and delicious. The prices are reasonable and the service is great. This is usually my go-to place when I want to eat out with my family.

I love Ocean Star because of:

  • Their burger and fries.
  • They are affordable.
  • Overall atmosphere and aesthetic.
